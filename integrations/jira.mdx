title: "Jira"
---

Learn how to connect your Jira account to Refacto in just a few simple steps.

Follow these simple steps to connect your Jira account to Refacto.

<Steps>
  <Step icon="window" title="Open Integrations Page">
    Go to [app.refacto.ai/integrations](https://app.refacto.ai/integrations).\
    Here you’ll see all available integrations .

    <img
      src="/images/installation/integrations-page.png"
      alt="Integrations Page"
      className="hidden dark:block"
    />
  </Step>
  <Step icon="jira" title="Connect Jira">
    Click on **Connect** button to start linking your Jira account

    <img
      src="/images/installation/connect-github.png"
      alt="Connect GitHub Button"
      className="hidden dark:block"
    />

    <img
      src="/images/Screenshot2026-02-13at5.15.29PM-1.png"
      alt="Connect GitHub Button"
      className="dark:hidden hidden dark:block"
    />
    <img
      src="images/Screenshot2026-02-13at5.15.29PM.png"
      alt="Connect GitHub Button"
      className="dark:hidden dark:hidden"
    />
  </Step>
  <Step icon="key" title="Authorize via Atlassian">
    After clicking Connect, you will be redirected to the Atlassian authorization page.\
    Refacto AI will request access to your Jira account

    <Frame>
      ![Image](/images/image-3.png)
    </Frame>
    Review the permissions and click **Accept** to grant access.
  </Step>
  <Step icon="check" title="Successful Connection">
    Once you click **Accept**, your Jira account will be successfully connected to Refacto.

    You will see a confirmation message:

    - Successfully Connected!
    - Your Jira account has been successfully connected to Refacto.

      <Frame>
        ![Image](/images/image-5.png)
      </Frame>
      \
      You will automatically be redirected back to the Refacto dashboard.
  </Step>
  <Step icon="plug" title="Verify Integration Status">
    After redirection:

    - Go back to the **Integrations** page.
    - You will now see Jira marked as **Connected**.
    - A connected indicator will appear beside Jira.

      <Frame>
        ![Image](/images/image-6.png)
      </Frame>

    This confirms that your Jira account is successfully integrated with Refacto.
  </Step>
  <Step icon="party-horn" title="You're All Set!">
    Your Jira integration is now active.  \
    Refacto can now fetch Jira data for analytics, reports, task tracking, and investment insights.
  </Step>
</Steps>
